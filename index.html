About us

Spark Foundation NGO

We are a non-profit organisation working for the betterment of the poverty in India. We provide various services like free education, free food & grains, employment, etc to the poor people in India. Our main aim is to provide all the facilities to the poor and give them employment oppurtunities to help them make a living, this way the poverty in India can be reduced and the employment will be increased. We take donations from the people who willingly wish to help the poor and serve for the betterment of the country. All the donations are taken in the company's official bank account and are only used for the NGO purposes.

Our Services

Food

We provide free food and grains to
the people in need. We even provide free
gas cylinders to the families who are
under poverty line. It is our aim
that no one should sleep hungry.

Education

We provide free education to children
under the age 16. Our NGO provides
educations funds for students from age
16-23. We even take care of the books and
others items required for studying.

Business

We setup small scale businesses and
train the mens and womens of the family
for the business. These businesses provide
employment to many people. This way we are
trying make the poor families self sufficient.
